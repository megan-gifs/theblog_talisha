Japanese Indie/Rock
I had curated this playlist over a few minutes after finding mismatched Japanese rock songs all over my liked songs. I had realized I really enjoyed this genre of Japanese rock specifically called "Math rock" I always find it interesting how a genre of music can connect to a school subject. I enjoy the rapid guitar strums and class drum beats that keep the heart going. This is the playlist I recommend for a summer day walking down San Francisco. Keep in mind as the city's weather is chaotic, weird, and not very nice-- this playlist can still be played in even the rainiest of days. It's just another reason to keep your hopes up and possibly brighten your day.

Recommendations!:

- Nakaniwa no Shoujotachi by Shishamo
- New Stranger by sora tob sakana
- Telecaster Stripes by POLKADOTS STINGRAY
- Idea by Origami JP
- LOVE TRIP! by the peggies
