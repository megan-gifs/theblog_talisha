The Boogie
Suprisingly, this is an album I rarely use as I don't find much places to bust down an epic move out of nowhere. Therefore, this is a playlist that would either reside in my room or in the shower. I don't trust myself to listen to these songs in my car as I may end up in a ditch after banging my head to some Pitbull or taking my hands off the wheel to dance Usher or Rihanna. Overall, try this playlist when the classics are needed to be felt again. Once they start playing Jason Derulo at the clubs again, let me know.

Recommendations!:

- Seven Days in Sunny June by Jamiroquai
- Feels Good by Tony! Toni! Tone!
- Move Ya Body by Nina Sky, Jabba
- Want to Want me by Jason Derulo
- Burn This Disco Out by Michael Jackson
